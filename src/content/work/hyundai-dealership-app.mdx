            ## The Idea

            \
            Everyone appreciates the cultural aversion to the car purchasing experience. The Hyundai Dealers App is an advanced comparison centre resource that adds trust and transparency to a historically challenging interaction. Built as a sales tool for Hyundai dealers, this native Android application facilitates shared-screen dialogue to discover model lineups, discuss pricing and features, and review financing and promotional options. The most impressive feature, however, is the comparison tool that pulls the most up-to-date information from the JATO API to provide a comparison between any Hyundai model and any comparable competitor model on the market.
